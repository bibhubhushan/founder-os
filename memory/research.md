# Research — AI Educators & Builders

Compiled Feb 2026. Reference for learning paths, teaching philosophy, and building approach.

## Key People to Learn From

### Andrej Karpathy — Build From Scratch
- YouTube: @AndrejKarpathy (1.24M subs)
- GitHub: github.com/karpathy (133K followers)
- Founded Eureka Labs (July 2024)
- Coined "vibe coding" (Feb 2025)
- **Must watch:** Zero to Hero playlist, "Let's build GPT" (6.8M views), "Deep Dive into LLMs" (5.5M views)
- **Must read:** "Unreasonable Effectiveness of RNNs", "Software 2.0", "A Recipe for Training Neural Networks"
- **Key repos:** nanoGPT (53K stars), llm.c (29K stars), micrograd (15K stars), minbpe (10K stars)
- **Philosophy:** First principles, spelled out, minimal dependencies, progressive complexity

### Andrew Ng — Democratize AI Education
- Founder: DeepLearning.AI, Coursera, Landing AI, AI Fund ($370M+)
- Amazon Board member (2024)
- **Must take:** ML Specialization → Deep Learning Specialization → Agentic AI short courses
- **Must read:** The Batch newsletter, Machine Learning Yearning (free), AI Transformation Playbook (free)
- **Philosophy:** Intuition over formalism, practical/applied focus, structured paths, free access

### Others Worth Following
- **Jeremy Howard** (fast.ai) — Top-down teaching, code-first. course.fast.ai
- **Sebastian Raschka** — "Build a Large Language Model (From Scratch)" book, "Ahead of AI" newsletter
- **Yann LeCun** — World models, JEPA, left Meta for AMI Labs (Nov 2025). @ylecun
- **Geoffrey Hinton** — Nobel Prize 2024, AI safety advocate
- **Fei-Fei Li** — ImageNet creator, World Labs (spatial AI), "The Worlds I See" book
- **George Hotz** — tinygrad, hacker ethos, Twitch livestreams. github.com/geohot
- **3Blue1Brown** — Visual math intuition. Neural Networks + Essence of Linear Algebra series
- **Yannic Kilcher** — Paper breakdowns on YouTube
- **Jim Keller** — Chip design legend, Tenstorrent CEO

1. 3Blue1Brown — visual math foundations
2. Andrew Ng — ML Specialization on Coursera
3. fast.ai — Practical Deep Learning for Coders
4. Karpathy — Zero to Hero (build from scratch)
5. Sebastian Raschka — Build an LLM From Scratch
6. Yannic Kilcher / Two Minute Papers — stay current
7. Lex Fridman Podcast — depth and context
